Indie pop is a subgenre of alternative rock that originated in the United Kingdom in the mid-1980s, with its roots in Scottish post-punk bands on the Postcard Records label in the early 1980s (Josef K and Orange Juice) and the dominant UK independent band of the mid-1980s, the Smiths.

Smallpools

Smallpools is an US-based indie pop band that formed in 2013. The band consists of Sean Scanlon, Mike Kamerman, and Beau Kuther.

COIN

Coin, stylized COIN, is an American indie pop band formed in 2012 at Nashville, Tennessee. It currently consists of Chase Lawrence, Ryan Winnen, Joe Memmel and Zachary Dyke.

The Neighbourhood

The Neighbourhood is an American rock band formed in Newbury Park, California in 2011. The band is composed of vocalist Jesse Rutherford, guitarists Jeremy Freedman and Zach Abels, bassist Mikey Margott, and drummer Brandon Alexander Fried.

Handsome Ghost

Handsome Ghost is the solo project of American musician Tim Noyes. The musical style could be described as haunting yet beautiful synth and vocals, among other various digitalized instruments.

The Heirs

The Heirs are an LA indie-pop band consisting of members Brandon Hudson, Alex Flagstad, Savannah Hudson, Brennan Benko, Eian McNeely.
