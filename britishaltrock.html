British Alternative Rock

Click here to go back to the homepage British Alternative Rock- Alternative Music Dictionary

A less intense version of rock and a more intense version of pop. Typically strays from the generic lyrics of pop. Lots of sub-categories, but this category is focused on the bands from Great Britain.

Catfish and the Bottlemen

Catfish and the Bottlemen are an English rock band, formed in Llandudno, North Wales in 2007. Their debut album, The Balcony, reached number 10 in the UK Albums Chart and achieved Platinum status on 30 December 2016.

The 1975

The 1975 are an English rock band originating from Manchester. The group consists of lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist Matthew "Matty" Healy, lead guitarist Adam Hann, bassist Ross MacDonald, and drummer George Daniel.

Pale Waves

Pale Waves are a female fronted band from the U.K. with four singles released to date. They are often seen collaborating with members of The 1975 and are preparing for their first full length release in the coming months.

Black Foxxes

Black Foxxes are a three piece band fronted by Mark Holley that have gained an impressive following the last few years with their debut full length album, "I'm Not Well".

The XCERTS

The XCERTS are a three piece rock band originally formed in Aberdeen, Scotland, United Kingdom. They have released three studio albums, with their latest, There Is Only You, peaking at #96 on the UK Albums Chart.
